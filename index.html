About

A little bit about me

I'm a pre-medical student at Rutgers University studying Cell Biology & Neuroscience, with a minor in Business Administration. I'm driven by a genuine curiosity about human health — and a conviction that the best physicians are those who understand both the science and the systems around it.

My path has taken me through clinical environments, hospital corridors, research labs, and startup offices. Each experience has sharpened how I think about medicine, evidence, and impact.
